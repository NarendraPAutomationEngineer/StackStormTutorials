Two types of CLI's:
   st2ctl -h
   st2 -h
--------------------------

There are two ways to login into StackStorm:
	WebUI  (http://hostname:80/ )
	Command Line Interface(CLI) (st2 login st2admin –p <password>

How to change password of stackstorm ?

Step1: login into root
Step2: htpasswd /etc/st2/htpasswd st2admin
       Provide new password and also confirm it.
